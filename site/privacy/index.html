Privacy Policy

This policy explains how Companote handles information when you use the iPhone app.

Effective date: July 19, 2026

TürkçeDeutschFrançaisEspañolItaliano

1. Who we are

Companote is developed and published by SefyStudio ("SefyStudio", "we", "us"). This policy applies to the Companote mobile application.

2. Our privacy approach

Companote is designed to work without an account. We do not operate analytics, advertising, tracking, or app-data collection services in Companote, and we do not receive your companion profiles, saved photos, notes, reminders, weight entries, or care records.

3. Information stored on your device

Depending on the features you use, Companote may store information you enter or choose to save, including:

  • companion profile details and an optional profile photo;
  • scan photos, selected visual effects, dates, regions, and notes;
  • care reminders, completed care records, weight entries, and identity-card information;
  • app preferences and the local status needed to unlock Lifetime Pro.

These records are stored in the app's private storage on your device. Live camera previews are not recorded automatically. A photo is retained only after you choose to save it.

4. Device permissions

  • Camera: used only when you start a scan or choose to take a profile photo.
  • Photo Library: used only when you choose an existing image for a profile or another supported import action.
  • Notifications: used to schedule care reminders locally on your device. Reminder content is not sent to SefyStudio.

You can change these permissions at any time in iOS Settings. Some features will not work without the relevant permission.

5. Purchases

Lifetime Pro purchases are processed by Apple through the App Store and StoreKit. We do not receive or store your full payment-card details. Apple may process purchase, account, device, and transaction information under Apple's own privacy policy. Companote reads the purchase status supplied by Apple to unlock Pro features and restore purchases.

6. Reports, sharing, and encrypted backups

When you create a PDF, export a record, or make an encrypted backup, Companote creates the file only after your action and presents the iOS share sheet. Backups are protected with the passphrase you choose. SefyStudio does not receive that passphrase and cannot recover it.

Once you share or save an exported file outside Companote, the selected destination and its provider control that copy. Review the privacy practices of any service you choose before sharing. Avoid sending sensitive information through public channels.

7. Retention and deletion

Local records remain on your device until you delete individual content, use Companote's data-deletion controls, or remove the app. Deleting Companote cannot remove copies you previously exported to Files, email, cloud storage, or another app. An App Store purchase remains associated with your Apple account and may be restored after reinstalling.

8. Security

Companote uses iOS app-private storage and protects exported backup archives with encryption. No method of storage is completely risk-free. Protect your device with a passcode, keep iOS updated, and use a strong, unique backup passphrase.

9. Children

Companote is a general companion-care organizer and is not specifically directed to children. Because we do not collect app data, we do not knowingly collect personal information from children through Companote.

10. Health information and medical disclaimer

Companote organizes user-created visual records and care routines. It does not automatically detect parasites, wounds, or skin conditions; provide a diagnosis or health assessment; or recommend treatment. Contact a qualified veterinarian when you have a health concern.

11. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy to reflect product, legal, or operational changes. The effective date above will change when a revised policy is published.

12. Contact

Questions about this policy can be sent to online.reflectlb@gmail.com or through the official Companote Support page. Please do not include backup passwords, payment information, or other sensitive content.
