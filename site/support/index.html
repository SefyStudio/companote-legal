Help center

How can we help?

Quick fixes for the most common Companote questions. Companote currently supports iPhone.

For health concernsCompanote is not a diagnostic service. Contact a veterinarian if your companion may need medical attention.

The camera or a scan will not start

Open iPhone Settings → Privacy & Security → Camera and allow Companote. Then close and reopen the app. A development build also needs an active Expo development server; the App Store/TestFlight build does not.

A reminder notification did not arrive

Open Settings → Notifications → Companote, enable Allow Notifications, and check Focus/Scheduled Summary settings. In Companote, confirm that the reminder is enabled and its date and time are still in the future. Local reminders do not require a Companote account.

The Lifetime Pro price is unavailable

Confirm that you are online and signed in to the App Store, then reopen the Pro screen. The localized price comes directly from Apple and may not appear in a development build before the in-app purchase is available for testing.

Restore Lifetime Pro

Use Restore Purchase on the Companote Pro screen while signed in with the Apple Account that made the purchase. A non-consumable purchase is not charged again. If restoration still fails, restart the iPhone and try once more.

Back up or move your records

Create an encrypted backup in Companote, choose a strong passphrase, and save the exported file somewhere you control. To move records, import that file on the other installation and enter the same passphrase. SefyStudio cannot recover a forgotten passphrase.

Delete your data

Use the deletion controls in Companote to remove individual records or all app data. Uninstalling the app removes its private local storage, but it does not delete PDF or backup files you previously exported elsewhere.

Before contacting support

Have your Companote version, iPhone model, iOS version, app language, and a short sequence of steps ready. Never send a backup passphrase, payment details, private companion records, or unredacted medical information.

Contact

Email sefystudio@gmail.com for app issues, feedback, or feature requests. Do not send backup passwords, payment details, or other sensitive information.
